Our Mission

To provide hope, care, and opportunities for orphaned and vulnerable children in Kuria, Migori County, empowering them to lead successful lives and break the cycle of poverty.

Our Programs

Providing education to underprivileged children in Kuria.

Offering healthcare services to vulnerable families.

Empowering women through skill development programs.
